OUR VISION

Our vision is to make AMCOSA a center excellence in science education in Nigeria. Academically strong and one of the top 50 schools in the country. Our top priority is to rebuild, renovate and maintain the Physics, Chemistry and Biology laboratories.Exposing students to Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics

Mission Towards a Center of Excellence.

Leverage Technology – Open the School Community to plethora of information and knowledge resource. Leverage Old Students’ Network – Spreading & Sharing the Tasks. Incorporate Elements of Sustainability.
STEERING AMCOSA TOWARDS THE FOLLOWING GOALS
  • Creating a worldwide community among the alumni body and facilitating opportunities for lifelong engagement with the school.
  • Creating a culture of philanthropy among the alumni body, to ensure that alumni continue to contribute their time, talent and financial resources to the betterment of the school.
  • To increase alumni participation and involvement in school activities
  • To promote pride, tradition, and loyalty among both current students and former students
  • enhancing communication between and among the College community, alumni and friends.
